The Annotating Pocket:


What Is It?


This for our final project in Stem! We have decided to create an annotating tool that helps people keep their annotating materials together. This tool will be made using 3D tools and Onshape.


The tool has a area for your highlighters and a special mini cabinet for the annotating tabs. The front cover is based on the classic disney story tale design.
